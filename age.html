How the Age Calculator Works

This calculator measures your exact age down to the day, not just in years. It's useful for filling out forms that require precise age, checking eligibility for age-restricted services, or just satisfying curiosity about how many days you've been alive.

Why count days? Knowing your total days alive can be surprisingly useful: tracking health milestones, calculating insurance rates, verifying age for visa applications, or celebrating a "10,000 day birthday."

How accurate is it? The calculator accounts for leap years and varying month lengths. It calculates the exact difference between your birth date and today's date, accurate to the day.
