Tipping Etiquette Around the World

Not sure how much to tip? You're not alone. Tipping customs vary dramatically by country, and getting it wrong can be awkward. This calculator helps you split any bill with any tip percentage.

Common tipping guidelines:

  • United States: 15-20% is standard for restaurant service. 10% for takeout or buffets.
  • Canada: Similar to the US — 15-20% at restaurants.
  • United Kingdom: 10-15% is typical, but check if a "service charge" is already included on the bill.
  • Australia: Tipping is optional and not expected. Round up or tip 5-10% for exceptional service.
  • Japan: Tipping is not customary and may even be refused. Exceptional service is considered part of the job.
