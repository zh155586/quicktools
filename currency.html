Currency Conversion Made Simple

Whether you're traveling abroad, shopping on international websites, sending money overseas, or just curious about exchange rates, this converter gives you a quick estimate of what your money is worth in another currency.

Which currencies are supported? US Dollar (USD), Euro (EUR), British Pound (GBP), Japanese Yen (JPY), Australian Dollar (AUD), and Chinese Yuan (CNY). These cover most common travel and business needs.

Are these live exchange rates? The rates are updated regularly but are approximate. Banks and currency exchange services may charge fees or offer slightly different rates. For exact rates, check with your bank or a service like Wise or Revolut.

Travel tip: When traveling, avoid exchanging currency at airports — their rates are usually the worst. Use a local ATM or a travel card for better rates.
